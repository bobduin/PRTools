im_center

IM_CENTER

Fixed mapping shifting blobs to center of gravity

    B = IM_CENTER(A)
    B = A*IM_CENTER

The objects in the binary images are shifted such that their centers of  gravities are in the image center.

    B = IM_CENTER(A,N)

In all directions N rows and columns are added after shifting.  Default N=0.
