MARKCOLS

Randomly change marker colors in lineplot or scatterplot

COLS = MARKCOLS get colors of lines and markers  MARKCOLS(COLS) set colors of lines and markers  COLS = MARKCOLS(SEED) randomly rotate colors of lines and markers

COLS should have 3 three columns. SEED is an integer scalar.
