GETLABELS

Get labels field in mapping

       LABELS = GETLABELS(W)

LABELS will be used for assigning feature labels (FEATLAB) to the  output dataset D of a mapping D = A*W, or D = PRMAP(A,W). Note that  the labels of D are the labels of the dataset A in this example.
